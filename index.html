Our Story

You already know these aromas.

The cardamom in your mother's chai. The spice that filled your grandmother's kitchen on a slow Sunday. You can close your eyes and smell them right now — because some aromas don't just season a dish. They hold a moment, a person, a place you can't quite return to.

A cup remembered

The first sip of chai that tastes exactly like home, no matter how far you've travelled.

A kitchen that hums

The warmth of a pot on the stove and hands that knew every spice by heart.

A table shared

The meals that gathered everyone close, where flavor was really just love, plated.

We started Bare Botany to protect those moments — to bring these treasured ingredients to your table with the purity, care and authenticity they deserve. So the aromas that made your memories can go on to make new ones.
